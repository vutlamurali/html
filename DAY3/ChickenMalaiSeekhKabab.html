Chicken Malai Seekh Kabab

                           To Make Chicken Malai Seekh Kebab

Malai Seekh Kebab Recipe: in a delicious melange of tangy spices, rich cream and succulent mince chicken with these drool-worthy restaurant-like seekh kebabs! Pair it up with mint chutney and onion rings.

            Ingredients of Chicken Malai Seekh Kebab
            500 gms chicken mince
            4 tsp cream
            1 tsp dried fenugreek leavesSalt (as per taste)Chaat masala (as per taste)
            1 tsp black pepper (freshly ground)
            3 tsp ginger-garlic paste
            1 large onionBread crumbs (as per requirement)
