4. Chicken Popcorn

                         To Make Chicken Popcorn

Chicken Popcorn Recipe: Bite-sized chicken popcorns are just perfect as appetizers for a dinner party. Full of spices, chillies and fried to perfection. Chicken popcorn is the finger snack that you can easily prepare at home for kids or for a party

       Ingredients of Chicken Popcorn
       500gms chicken (boneless)
       2 tbsp corn flour or all purpose flour
       1 egg1 cup bread crumbs
       1/4 tsp salt
       3/4 tbsp ginger garlic paste
       1/4 tsp black pepper
       1/2 tsp onion powder (optional)
       3/4 tsp red chilli powder or paprika or cayenne pepperoil for deep frying
